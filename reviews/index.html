FAQ

How do you decide the “best” AI girlfriend or AI boyfriend app?

We score across six pillars: chat realism, roleplay depth & memory, persona/behavior controls, voice quality, safety (SFW/NSFW), and value. Apps that keep characters “in persona” over long chats and offer clear boundaries rank higher.

What’s the difference between an AI companion and an AI character platform?

Companion apps focus on caring daily conversation (wellness, affection). AI character platforms add deeper roleplay tools—persona editing, memory notes, and behavior rules—for complex scenes and multi-chapter stories.

Do you test voice features?

Yes. We evaluate real-time voice for natural tone, latency, and alignment with the character’s personality. Strong voice presence improves immersion in romantic and roleplay scenarios.

Are paid plans worth it?

Usually yes if you chat often. Premium plans unlock longer memory, advanced editors, higher message limits, and better voice—key for believable AI girlfriend/boyfriend experiences or serious AI character roleplay.

Is NSFW allowed?

UIt depends on the platform. Our reviews clearly mark SFW/NSFW policies, adult-only gates, and the quality of safety/boundary controls (age checks, filters, opt-in toggles).

Which app has the best long-term memory for roleplay?

Look for platforms that store and apply long-term memory plus behavior rules. Those consistently keep the character on script across long arcs and complex relationship development.

How private is my data?

We read each privacy policy and note storage, logging, and deletion options. Choose apps with transparent data handling, optional local controls, and clear NSFW policies for adult users.
